## 摘要
自动驾驶需要在动态交通环境中进行长时域闭环决策。潜在世界模型通过在紧凑的潜在空间中进行基于想象的决策，为此问题提供了有效框架。然而，多源观测包含与控制无关的冗余信息，而可靠的驾驶决策依赖于风险相关关系、未来动态以及连续动作调整。这种不匹配使得观测重建和绝对动作建模在学习决策相关的潜在动态方面表现次优。我们提出LIDAR-AD，一种用于自动驾驶的无解码器潜在交互梦想家与动作残差链。LIDAR-AD用降冗余的潜在对齐替代观测重建，促进多源驾驶输入中风险相关关系的紧凑表示。它进一步将车辆控制建模为残差动作更新，并使用残差动作序列对比学习来将多步残差驱动的 rollout 与未来潜在状态对齐。确定性分析表明，潜在-tanh残差参数化保持了内部动作可达性，同时将平滑的长时域控制表示为紧凑的局部更新。这些设计共同改进了风险感知的状态抽象、连续控制建模以及长时域动态预测。在多种模拟驾驶场景中的广泛实验表明，LIDAR-AD持续优于世界模型基线，在基于学习的方法中获得了最高奖励和最佳成功率。基于nuPlan的日志重建场景的评估进一步证明了LIDAR-AD在现实交通布局下的可迁移性。

## Abstract
Autonomous driving requires long-horizon closedloop decision making in dynamic traffic environments. Latent world models offer an effective framework for this problem by enabling imagination-based decision making in compact latent spaces. However, multi-source observations contain controlirrelevant redundancy, whereas reliable driving decisions rely on risk-relevant relations, future dynamics, and continuous action adjustments. This mismatch makes observation reconstruction and absolute action modeling suboptimal for learning decisionrelevant latent dynamics. We propose LIDAR-AD, a decoderfree Latent-Interaction Dreamer with Action-Residual Chains for autonomous driving. LIDAR-AD replaces observation reconstruction with redundancy-reduced latent alignment, encouraging compact representations of risk-relevant relations in multi-source driving inputs. It further models vehicle control as residual action updates and uses residual-action sequence contrastive learning to align multi-step residual-driven rollouts with future latent states. A deterministic analysis shows that the latent-tanh residual parameterization preserves interior action reachability while representing smooth long-horizon control as compact local updates. Together, these designs improve risk-aware state abstraction, continuous-control modeling, and long-horizon dynamics prediction. Extensive experiments across diverse simulated driving scenarios demonstrate that LIDAR-AD consistently outperforms world-model baselines, achieving the highest reward and the best success rate among learning-based methods. Evaluations on nuPlan-derived log-reconstructed scenarios further demonstrate the transferability of LIDAR-AD under real-world traffic layouts.

## 2. 论文提出的方法论：核心思想、关键技术细节

### 核心思想
- 提出LIDAR-AD，一种无解码器的潜在交互梦想家（Latent-Interaction Dreamer），使用动作残差链（Action-Residual Chains）进行自动驾驶。统一在RSSM潜在状态$s_t=(h_t,z_t)$上优化三个互补模块：
  1. **Decoder-Free Latent Interaction Representation (DLIR)**：替代观测重建，通过风险指导的分组编码、成对交互融合和风险加权冗余减少目标，将RSSM后验状态与交互感知嵌入对齐。
  2. **Residual-Action World Model (RAWM)**：将连续控制参数化为在预tanh空间中的残差更新，使零残差保持原动作、任意目标可达、小残差产生平滑变化，并将执行动作和残差联合编码作为过渡模型输入。
  3. **Residual-Action Chain Contrastive Learning (ARC-CL)**：对K步残差动作链递归驱动的prior rollout与未来后验状态进行InfoNCE对比学习，使模型捕捉累积控制后果。

### 关键技术细节
- **DLIR**：将观测分为自车状态、LiDAR、导航组；计算确定性风险描述符（结合LiDAR距离衰减、横向/航向误差、速度/转向边界）；分别编码后通过门控成对交互融合；与RSSM后验状态进行风险加权Barlow Twins式对齐（对角项权重$\omega_i$，非对角项系数$\sqrt{\omega_i\omega_j}$）。
- **RAWM**：逆动作$u_{t-1}=\text{atanh}(\text{clip}(a_{t-1},-1+\epsilon,1-\epsilon))$；残差动作$\Delta u_t\sim\pi_\theta(\cdot|s^q_t,u_{t-1})$；执行动作$a_t=\tanh(u_{t-1}+\Delta u_t)$；联合嵌入$d_t=E_{\text{act}}([a_t;\Delta u_t])$输入RSSM过渡。理论分析表明零残差不变性、内部可达性、一阶泰勒展开给出局部紧凑性。
- **ARC-CL**：从当前后验状态$s^q_t$出发，用$K$步残差动作嵌入$d_{t+k}$递归应用过渡模型得到预测状态$\hat{s}^p_{t+K}$，与真实后验$s^q_{t+K}$进行InfoNCE对比（余弦相似度，温度$\tau$）。
- **总损失**：$\mathcal{L}_{\text{WM}}=\mathcal{L}_{\text{reward}}+\mathcal{L}_{\text{continue}}+\beta_{\text{dyn}}\mathcal{L}_{\text{dyn}}+\beta_{\text{rep}}\mathcal{L}_{\text{rep}}+\lambda_{\text{DLIR}}\mathcal{L}_{\text{DLIR}}+\lambda_{\text{ARC}}\mathcal{L}_{\text{ARC}}$，其中$\mathcal{L}_{\text{dyn}}$、$\mathcal{L}_{\text{rep}}$为RSSM的KL散度和后验正则化。
- **策略学习**：在想象轨迹上使用Dreamer风格的Actor-Critic优化，残差动作随状态一起更新。
